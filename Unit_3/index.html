Jesus Christ is the face of the Father's mercy

The central vision of the Catholic Worker Movement is to work for:

  • a new heaven
  • and a new earth,
  • wherein justice dwelleth.

This vision recognizes the primacy of the spiritual and the doctrine of the Mystical Body of Christ. The Catholic Worker is a new way of life involving Houses of Hospitality for the daily practice of the Works of Mercy... - Dorothy Day, The Catholic Worker, 1940.
